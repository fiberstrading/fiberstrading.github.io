WHO WE ARE

Mission

To be the preferred abaca fiber supplier in the Philippines

Vission

FIBERS TRADING INC. aspires to be the most reliable and robust abaca supplier in the Philippines. Our strategic sourcing positions make our production cost-effective while ensuring consistent quality of abaca fiber

Goal

To supply high quality abaca fibers to the international market to satisfy the needs for this natural raw material in the production of sustainable, biodegradable and environment friendly products.
